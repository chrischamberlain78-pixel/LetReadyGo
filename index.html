Who We Are

LetReadyGo™ is a professional property turnaround service specialising in end-of-tenancy cleaning and clearance for vacant homes. We work closely with landlords, letting agents, and property managers to ensure properties are left clean, compliant, and ready for inspection or re-letting.

One visit. One standard. Properties left clean, cleared, and let-ready.
Our Services

We deliver thorough, inspection-ready cleaning and clearance services for vacant and pre-move-in properties, with optional add-ons available where required.

  • End of Tenancy Cleaning (EOT)
  • Pre-Move-In Deep Cleaning
  • Property Clearance & Waste Removal
  • Carpet Cleaning & Extraction
  • Steam Cleaning (Kitchens & Bathrooms)
  • Appliance & Oven Cleaning
Complimentary Carpet Clean (One Room Included)

Each end-of-tenancy clean includes one complimentary carpet clean of choice. Applies to one standard-size room with a standard-condition or lightly soiled carpet. Additional rooms are quoted separately. Heavily stained or damaged carpets are not guaranteed.

Garden Tidy (Included)
  • Light grass cut and quick tidy
  • Light rubbish removal and sweep-down
  • No landscaping or heavy clearance

Heavy garden waste clearance can be quoted separately if required.
